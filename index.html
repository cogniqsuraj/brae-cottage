Getting Here

Winster is a picturesque village in the Peak District National Park, easily accessible from major cities:

  • Manchester: Approximately 1 hour drive
  • Sheffield: Approximately 45 minutes drive
  • Derby: Approximately 30 minutes drive
  • Nottingham: Approximately 1 hour drive

Parking: Private parking available on-site

Nearby Attractions

  • Historic Winster village center - walking distance
  • Local traditional pub - walking distance
  • Peak District walking trails - on your doorstep
  • Chatsworth House - short drive
  • Matlock Bath - nearby
  • Haddon Hall - close by
